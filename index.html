Group Update Memo

Thank you from Tobyn

Hi everyone, It’s great to be able to connect with you all on this new platform. I wanted to take a moment to say thank you and recognise some of the recent wins across the group.

Firstly, thank you for all your efforts in March. We delivered a record month for GP and finished above budget — a fantastic achievement. Your hard work and commitment are genuinely appreciated. A special mention to Station Tyres for being the furthest above budget — a great result.

Secondly, we completed our Totton expansion, our first greenfield site, and held a brilliant open day. Thank you to everyone who supported this. I know some of you have asked about new equipment at your sites — Totton was our test ground, and we will look to roll out more upgrades as part of our future plans.

Thirdly, we’ve taken over our 10th site — Youngs Service and Repair in Buckinghamshire. Connor, Colin and Marc are on-site this week supporting the team and showing them how we operate. We are building the best autocentre group in the country, and that only happens because of the effort each of you puts in every day.

Thank you again for everything you’re doing. Tobyn
